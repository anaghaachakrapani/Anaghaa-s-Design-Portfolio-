About

Hello, there! It’s great to see you!

I am Anaghaa Chakrapani, a Design & Research student based out of Mumbai & Ujjain. I aim to craft meaningful experiences with Design, Technology, Business and Psychology. Nice to meet you!👋🏽

Currently , I am an incoming UX Research Intern at Turian Labs, a Research and innovation strategy firm based out of Pune, India . I’m also a Third-Year Student at Avantika University, majoring in User Experience Design. Previously, I worked as an UX Design and Developer Intern at Ahhaa, a startup based in New York, USA. I have been a team lead for a live project where I lead a group of designers and engineers and also as a design volunteer for non profit ogranisations.

I have worked with local startups & foreign startups.. My favourite projects are the ones which involve heavy user research and creatiing a brand new experience for the consumers. I am an avid researcher and have presented my paper at the prestigious IEEE Smart Cities Conference 2020. I have also been selected in the WPP's NextGen Leader Programme for Summer 2021.

When I don’t have any deadlines, you can find me :
💻  Watching Anime or a TV show
🥾  Shoe Shopping
🈸  Learning a language
🖋  Creating content for my blog
📸  Exploring different places in my city
